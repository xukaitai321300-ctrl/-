## Description: <br>
华夏老祖宗智慧 - 用中华老祖宗的智慧框架帮用户分析决策、效率、成长、关系、搞钱、生活问题，包含24个经典智慧框架，每个有独特的思考工具和输出结构，支持跨模块组合应对复杂场景。 <br>

## Use Case: <br>
External users invoke this skill for structured coaching and analysis across decisions, productivity, personal growth, relationships, money, and life balance. The skill maps user situations to one or more Chinese classical wisdom frameworks and returns concise, stylized Markdown guidance with practical next steps. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Broad automatic triggers may route ordinary coaching or advice conversations into the skill's stylized framework. <br>
Mitigation: Invoke the skill explicitly when that voice and structure are desired, or disable broad automatic routing for normal conversations. <br>
Risk: The skill can produce opinionated guidance for high-stakes legal, medical, financial, career, or relationship decisions. <br>
Mitigation: Treat outputs as coaching support, not professional advice, and independently verify high-stakes recommendations before acting. <br>
