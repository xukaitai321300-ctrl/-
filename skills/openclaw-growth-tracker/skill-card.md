## Description: <br>
OpenClaw 成长追踪器 helps OpenClaw users locally track conversations, tasks, capabilities, efficiency, achievements, and daily or weekly growth reports. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
OpenClaw users use this skill to initialize and maintain a local growth log, then review task statistics, capability scores, efficiency trends, achievements, and periodic reports. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill may automatically record OpenClaw conversations or usage after setup without clear limits or stop/delete controls. <br>
Mitigation: Review before installing, pause or avoid tracking during sensitive conversations, and confirm that stored records can be inspected and deleted. <br>
Risk: The skill stores personal productivity and activity data locally under ~/.openclaw. <br>
Mitigation: Keep the local data file private, review retention settings, and delete or archive records according to the user's privacy needs. <br>
