Quality Score = (Clarity Score + Resolution Score + Relevance Score + Diversity Score) / 4

Where:
- Clarity Score (0-10): Image sharpness (Laplacian variance)
- Resolution Score (0-10): Meets minimum resolution (>= 1024x1024)
- Relevance Score (0-10): Matches collection target (product type + angle)
- Diversity Score (0-10): Diverse angles, lighting, backgrounds
