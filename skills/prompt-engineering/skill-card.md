## Use Case: <br>
Developers, creators, and external users use this skill to structure prompts for LLMs, image generators, and video models. It provides prompt patterns, examples, and CLI command snippets for improving AI output quality and consistency. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill recommends installing and authenticating the inference.sh CLI. <br>
Mitigation: Review the installer before running it, or use manual download with checksum verification from the published checksums file. <br>
Risk: Prompts may be sent to external model providers through the CLI examples. <br>
Mitigation: Do not include secrets, credentials, or sensitive private content in prompts submitted to external providers. <br>
