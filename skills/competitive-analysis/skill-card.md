## Use Case: <br>
External founders and solopreneurs use this skill to identify and tier competitors, gather public competitive intelligence, compare offerings, and turn market gaps into a positioning wedge. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Competitive research may involve sensitive business, customer, or account information. <br>
Mitigation: Use lawful public sources by default and include confidential information only when the user intentionally provides it for analysis. <br>
Risk: The skill may activate on broad competitor-related questions and produce a deeper analysis than the user expected. <br>
Mitigation: Confirm the research scope, target competitors, and desired depth before performing a full competitor review. <br>
