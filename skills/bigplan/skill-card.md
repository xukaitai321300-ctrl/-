## Description: <br>
BigPlan guides product research by analyzing market, technology, and supply-chain factors for a product direction and producing entry, standard, and flagship product specification options. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[kobenfang](https://clawhub.ai/user/kobenfang) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Product managers, founders, and hardware or product teams use this skill to turn a product direction into a structured research report covering market context, technical feasibility, supply chain, costs, pricing, schedule, risks, and three product option tiers. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Broad trigger wording may activate the skill during general product analysis requests. <br>
Mitigation: Confirm the user wants a BigPlan-style product research report before applying the workflow. <br>
Risk: Market, BOM, pricing, and schedule estimates may be approximate or stale. <br>
Mitigation: Validate generated estimates against current supplier quotes, market data, and internal engineering assumptions before making product decisions. <br>
